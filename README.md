# Eiffel Tower

## History
The Eiffel Tower (French: *La tour Eiffel*) is a wrought‑iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. Designed and built by the engineering firm of Gustave Eiffel, it was constructed from 1887 to 1889 as the centerpiece of the 1889 World's Fair. Originally intended as a temporary exhibit, it has become a global cultural icon and one of the most recognizable structures in the world. It was nearly demolished in 1909 but was saved due to its utility as a radio transmission tower.
